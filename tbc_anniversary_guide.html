üéÆ Major Gameplay Changes

Bloodlust/Heroism (Massive Change)

This is the biggest meta shift from original TBC!
  • Now RAID-WIDE instead of party-only (affects all 25 players)
  • Applies 10-minute Sated/Exhaustion debuff to entire raid
  • Debuff resets on boss kill or wipe - No more waiting between pulls!
  • One Shaman can now buff the entire raid

Attunements (Account-Wide)

Content Type First Character Alt Characters
Heroic Dungeons Must reach Revered reputation Purchase BoA key bag, only need Friendly
Raids Complete full attunement Automatically unlocked across account

Raid & Dungeon Difficulty

  • Tier 4 Raids: Karazhan, Gruul, Magtheridon - Released in post-nerf state
  • Heroic Dungeons: Using pre-nerf tuning for more challenge
  • Tier 5+: Difficulty still being evaluated based on community feedback

ü•Å Drums & Haste Mechanics

How Drums Work in Anniversary

Aspect Details
Effect +80 Haste Rating to party members for 30 seconds
Range Party-wide only (5 players, NOT raid-wide)
Cooldown 2 minutes (via Tinnitus debuff)
Stacks with Bloodlust? YES - Separate debuff systems
Can be chained? NO - Tinnitus prevents stacking
LW Required per Raid Only 1 per party (5 total for 25-man)
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: The Tinnitus debuff means Leatherworking is NO LONGER MANDATORY for all raiders. You only need one Leatherworker per 5-man party instead of the entire raid stacking it.

üí° Key Insights:
  • Jewelcrafting dominates early phases (Phase 1-3)
  • Blacksmithing scales best late-game with Epic gems
  • Gathering professions have NO combat bonuses (gold-making only)
  • You can drop and re-learn professions to optimize for different phases

üîÆ Casters

Mages, Warlocks, Shadow Priests, Elemental Shaman, Balance Druids

Primary Recommendations:

  1. Tailoring - Specialization sets
    • Frozen Shadoweave (Shadow damage)

  • Alchemy - Alchemist Stone, double flask duration
  • Engineering - Utility gadgets

  • Tailoring + Enchanting (classic combo)
  • Jewelcrafting + Alchemy (optimal late-game)

üíö Healers

All Healing Specs

Primary Recommendations:

  1. Alchemy - Double flask duration
    • Saves TONS of gold over the expansion
    • Best long-term investment
  2. Jewelcrafting - +60 SP or +81 Stamina

Secondary Options:

  • Tailoring - Primal Mooncloth set
  • Enchanting - +24 Healing on rings
  • Leatherworking - Drums (if needed)
Why Alchemy? Double flask duration means you'll spend half as much gold on consumables throughout the entire expansion. This adds up to thousands of gold saved.

üõ°Ô∏è Tanks

  1. Jewelcrafting - +81 Stamina (or mix)

    • Scales incredibly with Epic gems

Secondary Options:

  • Leatherworking - Drums + some tank gear (Druids)
  • Enchanting - Ring enchants for stats

Advanced Strategy: Profession Swapping

Many players use this strategy:
  1. Phase 1-2: Level Tailoring or Leatherworking
  2. Craft your specialization set (Shadoweave, Spellfire, etc.)
  3. Phase 2-3: Drop crafting profession
  4. Pick up Jewelcrafting or Engineering
  5. You keep the crafted gear, gain new profession bonuses

This requires gold investment but maximizes your power at each phase.
